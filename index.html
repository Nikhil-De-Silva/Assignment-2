History of Walmart

Walmart was started by SamWalton in 1962 with 1 store in Arkansas and today Walmart has nearly 12,000 retail stores. Walmart keeps itself true to its mission:Help people save money so they could live better. Walmart gets 260 million customers weekly and operates in over 28 countries.Walmart focused on a heavy acquisition strategy as it's revenue grew and acquired retail stores such as Mohr-Value and Kuhn's Big K. Walmart Operates in the supermarket and hypermarket indusries.
